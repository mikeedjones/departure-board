Pico 2 W quick specs

Compressed from Lesson 1.

RP2350 (the main chip)

SpecValue
CoresDual Cortex-M33 or RISC-V Hazard3, switchable at boot — MicroPython runs the Arm cores
ClockUp to 150MHz
SRAM520 kB, multi-bank
Flash4 MB, external QSPI + 16kB on-chip cache
GPIO exposed on board26 (23 digital-only, 3 also ADC-capable), 3.3V logic
PIO3 blocks, 12 state machines total — can emulate SD card, VGA, etc.
Fixed peripherals2×UART, 2×I2C, 2×SPI, 16×PWM, 1×HSTX

Wireless

ChipInfineon CYW43439 — separate from the RP2350
Link to RP2350SPI
Radios2.4GHz, single-band — 802.11n Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.2 (LE + Classic)
A plain "Pico 2" (no W) is the same RP2350 with no CYW43439 — no wireless at all.

Source: Raspberry Pi Pico 2 W Datasheet, Ch.1.
